## Overview

-This app will be used for users to send their packages inside their city and to other cities.
-Anyone who is registered and approved may become the currier. 
-Package delivery is paid by the reciever.
-Currier gets their payment as soon as the reciever confirms package delivery.
-Curriers may take many packages at a time and earn money by driving around the city.
-Approval must be legally confirmed and Terms of Use must be followed
